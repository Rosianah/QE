EDUCATION OF CALIBRE

Kenyan children have a right to education and not only education but quality education. The bills on children rights ensures this, yet the students living in rural areas do not enjoy this right due to their disadvantaged locations in rural areas. This project seeks to provide quality education to these disadvantaged areas by using technology to fill in the gap where teachers are not there or there are few teachers.

The solution

We will provide mobi stations which consists of a solar powered laptop, speakers, projector and a solar panel. The machines will be fitted with all the materials needed in te Kenyan curriculum. Teachers and students will be trained on basic computer skills to make it easy for them to operate the machine. A mobi machine is basically an artifical teacher who stands in the gap for the unavailable teacher.

Where?

Th epilot for this project will be Siaya County, specifically Sidok primary school. After successful implementation in Siaya County, Kenya, we will reach out to other counties facing the same issue.

Current statistics

35.0 Standard PTR according to TSC
53.6 PTR in Siaya County
63.9PTR in Sidok primary school
